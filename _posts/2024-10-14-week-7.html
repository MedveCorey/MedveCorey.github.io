After having watched this weeks screenings of the different skateboarding videos I began thinking about what things they reminded me of. While other games like Riders Republic or games from the Skate franchise for obvious reasons, Sunset Overdrive almost immediately came to mind. While it is clearly not a skateboarding game, I felt as if the game gives off a more general skater persona and feel that I often associate with skateboarding and skaters. The music in game provides a very upbeat and energetic feeling to the game. In addition with one of the main mechanics of that being on the use of movement like that of the ability to grind on things such as railing and powerlines. This ability lends the idea to the player to go out and mess around with it and try and chain from rail to rail or using other movement abilities such as wall running to keep there momentum going. This main movement mechanic is what made me think of it the most as i felt it was somewhat similar to that with skater and going out trying new and unique tricks that they could share with the world.
