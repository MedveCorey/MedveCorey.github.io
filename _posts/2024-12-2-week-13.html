In the past couple of days I started playing Marvel Rivals since it has just released and it has been a lot of fun. I am usually not the biggest fan of hero shooters but it has been quite fun to play in my experience so far. While many characters have some similarities to other characters in games like Overwatch 2 many of them are still rather unique and provide a fun experience with the destructible enviroment and the hero team up abilities. Jeff the Land Shark is probably been my favorite character to play thus far however there is still many I have yet to try.
