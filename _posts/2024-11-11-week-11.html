After watching The clips from Apocalypse Now specifically the Bridge scene it made me think somewhat of The Division Series. The lighting in the scene was quite beautiful especially with the chaos of the scene with the war going on. The Division and The Division 2 does this similarly at times, with the player exploring and essentially liberating parts of New York City and Washington DC in a world just struck by essentially a pandemic and where gangs and hostile groups running rampant throughout the cities. This experience gave me a similar feeling as to watching the bridge scene especially with the fact that you're exploring cities that are rather populated and often hard to move around in real life compared to in game where they are basically abandoned other than the gangs and survivor groups roaming the cities looking for supplies to survivor and cars sitting abandoned in the streets.
