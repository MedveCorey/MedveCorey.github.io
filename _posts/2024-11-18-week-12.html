In recent weeks while spending some free time scrolling through Tiktok and other social media I somehow managed to stumble into the Starlight Express section of them. While I don't often watch theater or musicals I instantly feel in love with this show just from the clips I have seen of it. The set and costume design is very futuristic and scifi themed. It also seems quite fitting with the recent discussion of Koyaanisqatsi as this show from what i gathered shows sort of the differences of technologies through different characters with trains with characters such as Greaseball, who is a diesel enginge, Rusty, a steam enginge, and Hydra, a hydrogen fuel car. Especially with Hydra's song Hydrogen which discusses the need for more renewable fuel sources both for trains and in the world at large.
